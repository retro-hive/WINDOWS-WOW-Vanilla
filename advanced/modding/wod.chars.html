Warlords of Draenor Models¶

WOD Models will change character models of your vanilla installation with models from the Warlords of Draenor extension.

Download¶

  • Official Link

Installation¶

  • Rename the file patch-3.mpq to patch-4.mpq

  • Copy the file patch-3.MPQ into the Data directory of your WoW installation.
