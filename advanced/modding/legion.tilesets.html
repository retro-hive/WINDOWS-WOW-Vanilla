Legion Tilesets¶

Legion Tilesets is a backport of the tilesets from Legion expansion into the Vanilla client.

Download¶

  • Official Link

Installation¶

  • Rename the file patch-3.MPQ to patch-5.MPQ

  • Copy the file patch-5.MPQ into the Data directory of your WoW installation.
